Elephanta Caves

Located on Elephanta Island, these rock-cut caves date back to the 5th to 8th centuries and are dedicated to Hindu gods, especially Lord Shiva.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST)

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, CST is an architectural marvel blending Victorian Gothic with Indian styles. It's one of Mumbai's busiest railway stations.
